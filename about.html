About Southern Precision Industries

Southern Precision Industries, headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, is a leading manufacturer of high-quality Jigs, Fixtures, Tools, Gauges, and Machining Components. With years of expertise in precision engineering, we provide innovative and cost-effective solutions tailored to meet the requirements of diverse industrial sectors.

Our commitment to quality, accuracy, and on-time delivery has earned us the trust of reputed clients across automotive, engineering, and manufacturing industries. By leveraging state-of-the-art technology, advanced machinery, and a skilled workforce, we ensure every product meets global standards.

At Southern Precision Industries, we believe in building long-term partnerships with our clients by focusing on customer satisfaction, continuous improvement, and sustainable manufacturing practices.
